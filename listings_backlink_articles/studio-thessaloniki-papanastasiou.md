## The Papanastasiou Neighbourhood

### What Surrounds You

Studio Thessaloniki Papanastasiou sits in a residential area where daily life is walkable and uncomplicated:

- **Supermarkets and marketplace:** Within 50 metres for groceries and household needs
- **Bakeries:** Fresh bread, koulouri, and pies from early morning, under €2
- **Cafes and tavernas:** Neighbourhood spots serving honest Greek food at local prices
- **Pharmacy:** On the same block
- **Bus stop:** Direct routes to both universities within walking distance

### The Residential Character

Eastern Thessaloniki around Papanastasiou is quiet without being isolated. The neighbourhood is home to families, professionals, and students who choose it for its calm streets and functional convenience. The weekly laiki agora, the street market with fresh produce at wholesale prices, adds colour and community on market days.

### Getting to Campus

Aristotle University is approximately twenty minutes by bus from the nearby bus stop. City College is accessible in a similar timeframe. The direct east-west bus corridor along Papanastasiou provides frequent service during term hours, with departures every five to eight minutes at peak times.
