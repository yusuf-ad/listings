# Student Housing Ano Poli

![Student housing Ano Poli spacious living room with two desks, a sofa, and natural light from the Ano Poli hillside](/images/guides/apartment-ano-poli-thessaloniki-student-accommodation-1.webp)

Every other listing in Ano Poli is a studio. One bed, one person, one routine. That works for most students, but not for the pair who arrived together, the friends who met during orientation and decided to save money by sharing, or the couple of classmates who prefer company to solitude. **Student housing Ano Poli** is the only two-bedroom flat in the Kleious building, and one of the few shared options in the entire upper town. Two bedrooms, two beds, a shared living room, a full kitchen with dishwasher and dryer, and a location at the gateway to Thessaloniki's most atmospheric neighbourhood. At €650 per month split between two tenants, that is €325 each for a level of space, equipment, and character that no studio in the city can match. **Student housing Ano Poli** is the social option in a building full of solo retreats.

## What Makes This Flat Unique

### Two Bedrooms, Two Lives

The flat is configured with two separate bedrooms, each with its own bed. This is not an open-plan studio with a curtain divider. You and your flatmate have private sleeping spaces, which means different schedules, different study hours, and different sleep patterns can coexist without friction. The shared spaces, the living room and kitchen, are where your routines overlap by choice.

### The Appliance Advantage

Student housing Ano Poli includes appliances that most Thessaloniki student flats consider luxury items:

- **Dishwasher:** Eliminates the most common source of flatmate tension
- **Dryer:** Practical during the brief rainy winter months and for students without balcony space
- **Washing machine:** In-unit laundry as standard
- **Oven:** Full cooking capability for proper meals
- **Private kitchenware:** Complete set of pots, pans, plates, and utensils
- **Air conditioning:** Cooling and electrical heating throughout
- **TV:** Shared entertainment in the living room
- **Wi-Fi:** Reliable internet included in the rent

### The Living Room

A proper living room changes how a flat functions. It provides a social space that is not the kitchen and not the bedroom. For two students sharing, this means you can host friends, watch something together, or simply sit in a common area without being in each other's private space. The living room furniture is included, so you arrive to a functional, comfortable shared area from day one.

## The Ano Poli Location

### Where the Upper Town Begins

The Kleious building sits at the beginning of Ano Poli, where the historic upper town meets the modern commercial centre. This position gives you the character of Ano Poli, the cobbled streets, the Byzantine walls, the Ottoman-era houses, while keeping you within a five-to-ten-minute walk of the city centre's shops, supermarkets, and bus routes.

### Daily Essentials

- **Supermarket:** Within 50 metres
- **Bakery and cafes:** Around the corner, open from early morning
