## Why Natural Light Matters

### The Productivity Connection

Research from Cornell University and the World Green Building Council shows that students and workers in naturally lit spaces perform 10-25% better on cognitive tasks than those in artificially lit environments. The bright student studio Ano Poli does not just feel pleasant, it actively supports better academic work.

Thessaloniki averages over 300 sunny days per year. A studio designed to capture this light delivers brightness for the vast majority of your stay. Even during the mild winter months, the sun angle in Thessaloniki is higher than in northern Europe, meaning the bright student studio Ano Poli receives usable natural light year-round.

### The Health Effect

Natural light regulates circadian rhythms, which govern sleep quality, mood, and energy levels. Students in bright living spaces report better sleep and more consistent daily routines, both of which are challenged during the adjustment period of an Erasmus exchange.
