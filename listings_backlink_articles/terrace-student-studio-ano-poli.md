# Terrace Student Studio Ano Poli

![Terrace student studio Ano Poli private terrace at sunset with a sweeping view over the Thessaloniki rooftops toward the gulf](/images/guides/terrace-student-studio-ano-poli.webp)

A terrace in Ano Poli is not the same as a terrace in the city centre. Down below, you see the building opposite. Up here, you see the city. The rooftops cascade toward the waterfront, the Thermaikos Gulf catches the evening light, and on clear days, the faint silhouette of Mount Olympus appears on the southern horizon. The **terrace student studio Ano Poli** puts this view behind a private door, accessible only to you, every morning, every evening, for the duration of your stay. Among the nine studios in the Kleious building, this is the one with the grand outdoor space, and five previous tenants have confirmed that the terrace is not just a feature, it is the reason you book. If outdoor living matters to you, the **terrace student studio Ano Poli** is the listing where inside and outside become one.

## The Terrace

### What You Get

The private terrace is a genuine outdoor living space, not a narrow balcony with a railing. The size accommodates a table, chairs, and the kind of use that turns a terrace into an extension of the studio:

- **Morning coffee:** Watch the city wake up from the hillside
- **Afternoon study:** Natural light and fresh air for productive reading sessions
- **Evening meals:** Cook inside, eat outside, repeat for eight months of the year
- **Weekend socialising:** Host two or three friends for a terrace dinner with a view

### The View

From Ano Poli's elevated position, the terrace looks out over the lower city. The rooftops of the commercial centre, the port cranes, the waterfront promenade, and the gulf beyond create a panorama that changes with the light. Sunsets turn the scene golden. Night reveals the city's scattered lights. This is not a sea-view apartment in the postcard sense, it is something better: a living position above the city that gives you perspective, both visual and psychological.
