# Student Apartment Ano Poli

![Student apartment Ano Poli with soft afternoon light, a tidy workspace, and a calm, minimally decorated interior](/images/guides/apartment-ano-poli-thessaloniki-erasmus-housing-6.webp)

Some students come to Thessaloniki for the nightlife, the social scene, the energy of a city that does not sleep until three in the morning. Others come to study. To read, to write, to think slowly in a quiet room while the neighbourhood outside settles into its own unhurried rhythm. The **student apartment Ano Poli** is for the second group. Described as a "calm 1-bed retreat," this apartment in the Kleious building delivers exactly what the name promises: a peaceful, private, wheelchair-accessible space where concentration is the default atmosphere. If your Erasmus semester depends on the quality of your study time, the **student apartment Ano Poli** provides the silence to support it.

## Why Calm Matters

### The Study Environment

Academic work requires sustained attention. A noisy flat, a busy street, or a thin-walled building with unpredictable neighbours all erode the ability to concentrate for long periods. The student apartment Ano Poli sits in a quiet residential street at the edge of Ano Poli, where traffic is minimal, nightlife is absent, and the dominant sounds are birdsong, distant church bells, and the occasional conversation from a neighbouring balcony. This is the acoustic environment in which essays get finished and exams get prepared for.

### The Neighbourhood Character

Ano Poli is the calmest central neighbourhood in Thessaloniki. The cobbled streets discourage through-traffic. The residential population values quiet. The tavernas close early by Greek standards. For students who struggle with noise, who need the evening hours for reading, who wake early for productive mornings, this neighbourhood matches their natural rhythm.

### Wheelchair Accessibility

The student apartment Ano Poli is one of two wheelchair-accessible units in the Kleious building. Accessible student housing in Thessaloniki is exceptionally rare, and this apartment provides a genuine option for students with mobility requirements who want to live independently in the historic upper town.

## Location

### At the Edge of Ano Poli

The Kleious building's position gives you the best of both zones:

- **Quiet living:** The calm of Ano Poli's residential streets
- **City access:** 5-10 minutes walking to the commercial centre
- **University reach:** Aristotle University 20 minutes on foot, City College by bus
- **Daily essentials:** Supermarket, bakery, and cafes within 50 metres

### The Commute

Students at the student apartment Ano Poli can walk to both universities. The route to Aristotle University runs downhill through the Roman Agora, a scenic twenty-minute walk. For the return uphill, buses are available on the main roads bordering Ano Poli.
