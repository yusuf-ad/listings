## The Location Advantage

The minimal studio Aristotelous Square places you on the most recognisable square in northern Greece. ### What Your Daily Walk Looks Like

Step out the door and you are on Aristotelous Square. The waterfront promenade is a two-minute walk south. Bakeries, supermarkets, and pharmacies are on every adjacent street.
